Leaf Blower Revolution - Idle Game

Leaf Blower Revolution is an idle game where you blow away leaves with nuclear leaf blowers, unlock upgrades, earn achievements, and more. Try out Leaf Blower Revolution now and watch numbers going up!

Description

Are you tired of blowing away leaves IRL? Or have you ever wanted to blow away the stupid leaves with a rocket engine filled with nuclear fuel? Then Leaf Blower Revolution is for you!

​

Purchase Autoblowers, unlock upgrades, earn achievements, and watch the numbers go up:

​

  • An idle game you can play actively or passively
  • Blow leaves virtually
  • Buy a variety of tools that help you blow away leaves more easily
  • Buy Autoblowers and watch them do the work while enjoying your coffee
  • Prestige, gain coins, and purchase powerful upgrades
  • Discover over 40 areas to find rare leaves
  • PETS
  • Watch numbers go up
  • And more leaf blowing!
